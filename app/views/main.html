MyJobs is a great tool to track, organize and manage your job applications and career advancement!

    Why you need it?
  • You dont want to apply for the same job twice!
  • You can save interview questions and great answers for future reference.
  • Keep track of your application status and see how close you are getting to a job offer!
  • Mark your achievement along the way so you have them at hand when you need to talk something great about yourself.

In order for this demo to work, you must enable pop-ups on this site. This is just so Dropbox can connect properly.Press the big green button to get started!
